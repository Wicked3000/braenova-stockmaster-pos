Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for a BraeNova StockMaster account, we collect the following information:

  • Your chosen shop name
  • Your owner username and securely hashed password
  • Any inventory, sales, and customer data (such as Dinau records) that you input into the system

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve the BraeNova StockMaster service. Your data is isolated to your specific shop ID and cannot be accessed by other shops.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your data is stored securely in our cloud database (Supabase), and all passwords are encrypted. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or shop data to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our software, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Contacting Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via our Support page.
